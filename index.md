# Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

## Information Collection and Use
Roll The Dice does not collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information directly.

## Advertising
This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and use certain non-personal information, such as device identifiers, cookies, and approximate location data, to serve ads. The information collected by Google AdMob is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from sites and apps that use its services here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

## Data Sharing
The developer does not sell, trade, or share personal user data. Any data collected for advertising purposes is handled by Google AdMob in accordance with their privacy policies.

## Children’s Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Google AdMob’s advertising services are configured in compliance with applicable child privacy regulations.
